Meatloaf

This is a great meatloaf recipe, it's easy to make and tastes delicious.

Meatloaf is a great main dish that brings smiles to everyone around the table Serve this with a side of mashed potatoes or mac and cheese for the perfect comforting meal.

When I find it difficult to create a menu for a holiday I look at this meatloaf recipe. It's easy to make on a special day and doesn't require much prep.

Ingredients

    Meatloaf Sauce

  • 1/2 cup chili sauce
  • 1/2 cup ketchup
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar

    • Meatloaf

  • 1/2 medium onion diced
  • 1 Tsp butter
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 3/4 cup panko
  • 2 lbs ground beef
  • 1 Tbsp ketchup
  • 1 Tsp Italion seasoning salt
  • 2 Tbsp chopped parsley
  • 1 Tsp salt
  • 1/2 Tsp black pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F. Line a rimmed baking pan with aluminum foil and spray with cooking spray.
  2. In a small pan, cook inions in butter over medium low heat until tender. Let them cool completely.
  3. In a medium bowl, combine eggs, milk, and breadcrumbs. Let the mixture sit for 5-10 minutes.
  4. Add the ground beef, cooked onions, 1 Tablesppon ketchup or chili sauce, Italian seasoning, parsley, and salt & pepper to the bowl. Mix until just combined.
  5. Form a 8"x4" loaf on the prepared baking pan and bake for 40 minutes.
  6. While the meatloaf is cookingm combine the chili sauce and ketchup (and brown sugar if using) to make meatloaf sauce. Spread mixture over the meatloaf and bake for an additional 15 - 30 minutes until cooked through and the meatloaf reaches and internal temperature of 160°F. Broil for 1 - 2 minutes if desired.
  7. Let the meatloaf rest for 10 minutesd before slicing and serving.
